# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and organizational trends. The dashboard enables HR teams and business stakeholders to identify factors influencing employee turnover and make data-driven decisions.

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard provides comprehensive insights into employee attrition and workforce performance. It helps answer key business questions such as:

- What is the overall attrition rate?
- Which departments experience the highest employee turnover?
- Which age groups are leaving the company most frequently?
- How does attrition vary by salary, gender, education, and job role?
- What is the average employee salary and tenure?

## 📌 Key Insights

- Overall employee attrition is **20.6%**.
- Sales Executives have the highest number of attrition cases.
- Employees aged **26–35** show the highest attrition.
- Most attrition occurs among employees earning **up to 5K**.
- Marketing and Life Sciences education backgrounds contribute significantly to attrition.
- Male employees have slightly higher attrition than female employees.
